# Forever Home Placement — Design Brainstorm

## Context
- Audience: Elderly individuals AND their adult children seeking eldercare placement
- Accessibility requirements: Large text (18px+ body), high contrast, generous spacing, simple navigation, no clutter
- Brand feel: Warm, trustworthy, compassionate, professional — like a caring family friend

<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement:** Warm Humanist — Soft Comfort & Trust
**Core Principles:**
- Generous whitespace and large readable text (min 18px body)
- Warm, calming color palette — never clinical or cold
- Simplified navigation with large tap targets
- Compassionate, human photography

**Color Philosophy:** Soft sage green (#5C8A6E) as primary — evoking nature, calm, and health. Warm cream (#FDF8F0) as background — gentle on aging eyes. Deep charcoal (#2C2C2C) for text — maximum readability. Dusty rose (#D4847A) as accent — warmth and care.

**Layout Paradigm:** Wide single-column sections with generous padding. No sidebars. Large section headers. Content blocks are simple and uncluttered. CTA buttons are extra large.

**Signature Elements:**
- Soft rounded corners on all cards and buttons
- Subtle warm drop shadows (no harsh borders)
- Gentle leaf/nature motifs as decorative accents

**Interaction Philosophy:** Forgiving and obvious. Large buttons with clear labels ("Call Us Now", "Request Free Consultation"). No hover-only interactions. No carousels that auto-advance.

**Animation:** Gentle fade-ins only. No fast motion. No parallax.

**Typography System:** "Merriweather" for headings (serif, warm, readable), "Open Sans" for body at 18px minimum. Strong size contrast between headings and body.
</idea>

## Selected Approach
**Chosen: Option 1 — Warm Humanist (Soft Comfort & Trust)**

Sage green + warm cream + dusty rose. Merriweather headings + Open Sans body at 18px+. Wide, uncluttered sections, extra-large buttons, gentle fade animations. Perfect for elderly users and their families.
