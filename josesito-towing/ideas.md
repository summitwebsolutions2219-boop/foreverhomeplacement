<idea>
**Design Movement:** Industrial Brutalism meets American Road Culture
**Core Principles:**
- Raw, heavy typography with high contrast
- Asymmetric layouts with bold diagonal cuts
- Utilitarian color palette anchored in steel and asphalt tones
- Trust signals front and center (24/7, fast response, licensed)

**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#1A1A1A) as the dominant background, with a punchy amber/yellow (#F5A623) as the action color — evoking caution tape, emergency lighting, and road signage. White text for readability. Rust red as a secondary accent.

**Layout Paradigm:** Diagonal section dividers (clip-path), full-bleed hero with overlapping text blocks, services in a staggered horizontal strip rather than a grid.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thick yellow diagonal stripes as decorative motifs (like road hazard markings)
- Oversized bold numbers for stats (e.g., "24/7", "15 min avg response")
- Heavy drop shadows on cards

**Interaction Philosophy:** Immediate, no-nonsense. Hover states are bold color swaps, not subtle fades. CTA buttons feel like physical buttons with press-down effects.

**Animation:** Entrance animations that slide in from the left (like a truck pulling up). Counters animate up on scroll. Section reveals are fast and decisive.

**Typography System:** "Bebas Neue" for headings (all-caps, condensed), "Source Sans 3" for body text. Hierarchy is extreme — H1 at 96px, body at 16px.

**Core Principles:**
- High-trust visual language borrowed from emergency services
- Clean white space balanced with strong color blocks
- Professional credibility through structured layout
- Mobile-first with thumb-friendly CTAs

**Signature Elements:**
- Orange accent bars on section headings
- Icon-forward service cards with subtle hover lift
- Testimonial strip with star ratings

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, confident transitions. Forms feel easy and frictionless. Every page section has one clear next action.

**Animation:** Subtle fade-up on scroll, smooth sticky nav transition, form field focus animations. Nothing distracting — the UI should feel calm and fast.

**Typography System:** "Barlow Condensed" for headings (semi-bold, impactful), "Lato" for body. Strong weight contrast between heading and body.

- Hand-drawn style icon outlines for services
- Warm testimonial cards with avatar photos

## Selected Approach
**Chosen: Option 2 — Modern Emergency Services (Clean Authority)**

Navy + Orange color scheme, Barlow Condensed + Lato typography, split-screen hero, sticky nav, and a prominent quote form. This communicates professionalism, speed, and trust — exactly what someone stranded on the road needs to see.
